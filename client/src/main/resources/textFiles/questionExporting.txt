Lecturers can export the questions and answers of a lecture to post FAQs on Brightspace or to share them with students.

-> During / At the end of the lecture, any lecturer/moderator can export all the questions and answers of that lecture, sorted by relevance (number of upvotes, answered questions).

-> Questions and answers will be exported to a file and it's name consists of the lecture name and the date and time it was exported.

-> To export questions and answers, click on the blue "Export Questions and Answers" button in the bottom right corner.

-> You can find all exported text files in a folder named "exportedQuestions".

-> Note: it is possible to export multiple text files during a lecture.
