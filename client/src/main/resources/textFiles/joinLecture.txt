To join a lecture, press the "Join Lecture" button on the main page.
Then, enter your name.

If you are a student, enter the lecture id in the second text field and press "Join Lecture".
If you are a moderator, select the "Are you a moderator" choice and enter the Mod Key in the third
    text field that just appeared. Press "Join Lecture" to finish your action.

If you want to go back, press the "Back" button.
